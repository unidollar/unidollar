FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is UniDollar?

UniDollar is the first Proof of Liquidity (PoL) and Proof of Stake token. 110 Million (88%) of the 125,000,000 supply was sent to Uniswap. No more tokens can ever be minted. A 2% tax is charged on each transfer which is automatically distributed as rewards to staked tokens. 15 Million (12%) of the supply has been reserved as a bonus reward for users that stake their tokens. These tokens will be distributed over a period of 12 weeks.

Any dApps planned for UniDollar?

The first dapp has been released with the token as a built in feature, the staking mechanism. The goal is to release more dapps, however the code is opened source and ANYONE is welcome to develop around the UniDollar token.
