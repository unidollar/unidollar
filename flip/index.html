    • You must have Metamask, TrustWallet (Mobile) or any compatible ERC20 browser wallet, PYRO Tokens and ETH for gas to play!
    • Your UNIUSD must be free and available for use. What you intend to play with must not be staked.
    • Buy a card using UNIUSD to become the owner of the card and set the timer for the round. Each time a card is purchased, the time is changed.
    • The card you own determines the amount of shares you have which determines the amount of dividends you receive from cards flipping.
    • 75% of the card purchase goes to the previous owner, 12.5% is distributed in divs to the other card owners, and 12.5% goes toward the jackpot.
    • The last person to buy a card with no buys after them before the timer runs out wins the jackpot.
    • There is a 10 minute cool down between each round and any player can click "Start New Round" to begin another round.
    • Card owners from the previous round remain as the card owners for the next round until a card is bought from them.
